### AccordionItemTrigger

AccordionItemTrigger renders a `button` and composes `Box`, this means you can
style it by passing the pseudo style props `_expanded`, `_disabled`, `_hover`,
etc.

### AccordionItemContent

AccordionItemContent renders a `div` and composes `Collapse` to provide the
height animation.
